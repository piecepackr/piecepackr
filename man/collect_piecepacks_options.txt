Usage: Program to arrange piecepacks


Options:
	-h, --help
		Show this help message and exit

	--author=AUTHOR
		Pdf author (default "")

	--filename=FILENAME
		Pdf filename (default "piecepack_collection")

	--keywords=KEYWORDS
		Pdf keywords (default "piecepack")

	--subject=SUBJECT
		Pdf subject (default "")

	--title=TITLE
		Pdf title (default "Piecepack collection")

	--decks=DECKS
		Comma separated list of decks to collect (default collects all of them)

	--paper=PAPER
		Pdf paper size, either "letter" or "A4" (default "letter")

	--font=FONT
		Default font family (default "sans")
